###### [[Formed elements]]: 
Erythrocytes = [[Red blood cells]] form the lower later of the centrifuged blood.
44% of a blood sample but 99.9% of the formed elements. 

[[Buffy coat]] = middle layer slightly gray-white later made of [[white blood cells | leukocytes]] (>0.01% of FE) and [[platelets]] (> 1% of FE).
less than 1%.
