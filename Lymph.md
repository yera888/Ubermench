Made of: Combination of interstitial fluid, solutes, immune cells, foreign material that enters the lymphatic vessels.

15% of interstitial fluid that doesn't get reabsorbed back into the vessels and goes into the venous end of the capillaries. 

Thats when a network of [[lymphatic vessels]] reabsorb the excess fluid and returns it to the venous circulation.

If this excess fluid wasn't removed it would cause body tissues to swell causing edema.
