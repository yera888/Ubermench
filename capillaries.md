Site of exchange between the blood and the [[alveoli]] of the lungs or between blood and systemic cells. 

They connect arterioles to venules 

Made of: tunica intima= a single layer of endothelium and a basement layer 

Group of capillaries = capillary bed
fed by metaarteriole which is a vessel branch of an arteriole.
smooth muscle ring called precapillary sphincter controls the blood flow to the true capillaries, when a tissue needs oxygen and nutrients the pprecapillary sphincter open and allow blood to fill throughout the capillary bed. They close when the tissues needs are meet.
