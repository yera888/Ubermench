Cancer in the [[leukocyte]] forming cells, abnormal development  of leukocytes in the bone marrow and circulating blood. As leukocytes increase in number erythrocytes and platelets decease in numbers because the cells take over the marrow and leave no room for other formed elements. 

As a result bleeding and anemia are the first signs of leukemia.

Acute leukemia = progresses fast if untreated death will happen after a few months of symptoms like anemia, hemorrhages and reoccurring infections. 

Chronic leukemia = progresses slowly survival exceeds 2 year from onset symptoms.
