These are the **most open** kind.

- They have **larger gaps**
    
- Their basement membrane is **discontinuous or missing**
    
- They let **larger materials**, like proteins, pass through
    

### Easy example

Imagine a **very loose fence with wide openings**.  
Now even bigger things can get through.

So sinusoids are used where the body needs to move **large substances**, not just tiny ones.
