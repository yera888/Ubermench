These are the **tightest** kind.

- The cells form a **complete lining**
    
- They are joined closely together
    
- Only small things pass through easily
    

The text says materials can pass by:

- **simple diffusion** = moving directly across
    
- **pinocytosis** = the cell sort of “drinks in” tiny droplets and carries them across
    

**Found in:** muscle, skin, thymus, lungs, and CNS

### Easy example

Imagine a **very fine screen door**.  
Only small things get through easily.

So continuous capillaries are good for places where the body wants **more control** over what leaves the blood.
