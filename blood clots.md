Severe trauma to a blood vessel causes the blood to coagulate or clot. 

In order for blood clots to form platelets form a plug which interacts with fibrin strands from the [[plasma]]. These strands capture RBC and platelets in the area to form a clot and if not they will circulate for 8-10 days broken down and recycled.

If too little platelets circulating in blood = thrombocytopenia
