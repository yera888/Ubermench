FUNCTION 

-Reduces friction in the joint during movement.

-Spongy cushion to absorb compression placed on the joint, preventing damage on the articulating ends of the bones.

> This special [[hyaline cartilage]] lacks a [[perichondrium]].

Since mature cartilage is [[avascular]] no nutrients can go to the cartilage. So if the cartilage is damaged tissue healing is delayed. HOWEVER repetitious compression and expansion that happens during exercise is vital to maintaining healthy articular cartilage because this it enhances its nutrition and waste removal.
