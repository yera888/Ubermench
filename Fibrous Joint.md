
>Most are immobile or slightly mobile, with no space between the articulating bones - meaning it has no joint cavity.

1. **[[Gomphoses]]: Only found in one place of the body which is in the alveolar processes of the mandible and maxilla.** 
2. **** [[Sutures]]: connects the skull bones together, fibrous joints that are immobile.**
3. **[[Syndesmosis]]**: Interosseous membrane between bones -> situated between bones. A sheet between joints.
