means *purse*

saclike structure that contains synovial fluid and is lined with a [[synovial membrane]]. Bursae happens around most synovial joints and also where [[bones]], [[Ligaments]], [[tendons]], muscle, and skin that overlie each other and rub together. 

>Can be connected to the joint cavity or completely separate. **They are made to alleviate the friction from body movements like tendons and ligaments rubbing against bone.**

An elongated bursa is called [[tendon sheath]].
