>*Chondro-* meaning cartilage

An articulation in bones that are joined by [[hyaline cartilage]] called **Synchondrosis** (singular), they are all immobile so classified functionally as [[Synarthroses]]. The main thing holding the bones together is cartilage. 

Epiphyseal plates in children's form Synchondroses that bind the epiphyses and the diaphysis of long bones. When the hyaline cartilage stops growing the bone replaces the cartilage and Synchondrosis no longer exists. ![[Pasted image 20260215191936.png]] 
The *Spheno-occipital Synchondrosis*  fuses between 18-25 and can assess the age of a skull.

EX:

growth plate (epiphyseal plate)

first rib–sternum joint (The costal cartilage)

**Costochondral Joints** -> The joints between each bony rib and its respective costal cartilage (different between the sternum and costal cartilage of ribs 2-7 which are synovial.)
