#### First STEP 

Begins with cell untwisting a segment of DNA that includes a region encoding a protein or protein-coding gene. 

The cell the uses the information in the gene to produce a protein in a process [[gene expression]]. 

To initiate the process of reading a gene a number of proteins bind to DNA molecules in the beginning of the gene sequence called [[promoter region]]

--->

Proteins move from the upstream end of the stream to the downstream end. 
There is a kind of [[gene regulation]] that happens at this point. Then an enzyme [[RNA polymerase]] assembles a new string of nucleotides that has a matching sequence of the template DNA during transcription. This new single-stranded molecule is [[RNA]] and its called [[mRNA]]. 

RNA polymerase continues to build an mRNA molecule until it reaches a sequence of bases (nucleotide sequence) in the gene that tells it to stop.

Once the process is complete the mRNA molecule can serve as a template for the construction of a protein.
