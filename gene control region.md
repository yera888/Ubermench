refers to an upstream section of DNA that includes the promoter region as well as other regulatory sequences that influence the transcription of DNA.

**Definition:** the local set of regulatory DNA that controls a gene.

- Always includes: [[Promoter]]
    
- Often includes: [[Enhancer]] / [[Silencer]]
    
- Sometimes bounded by: [[Insulator]]
    

**Key idea:** controls [[Gene expression]] mainly by controlling [[Transcription]].
