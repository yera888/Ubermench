when a cell **engulfs and eats** something, usually a germ, dead cell, or debris.(phago = eat)

- the [[phagocyte]] **recognizes** something unwanted
    
- it **surrounds** it with its membrane
    
- it **pulls it inside** into a little sac
    
- it **digests** and destroys it with enzymes
They function as APC for T- cells
