constructing a more complex thing from simpler parts.

---
**Biosynthesis** = cells building molecules using enzymes + energy.

- **Protein synthesis** = making a protein from amino acids (using mRNA, ribosomes, tRNA).
    
- **DNA synthesis** = building DNA from nucleotides (DNA polymerase).
    
- **RNA synthesis** = building RNA from nucleotides (RNA polymerase).
