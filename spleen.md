Largest lymphoid organ in the body monitors BLOOD for antigens.
Location: Upper left (Left for lymphoid)
Main job:
-initiates an immune response when antigens are found in the blood -> white blood function
-reservoir for [[Red blood cells]] and [[platelets]] -> red blood function
-[[phagocytosis|phagocytizes]] bacteria and other foreign materials from the blood -> red pulp function
-[[phagocytosis|phagocytizes]] old defective RBC and platelets -> Red pulp function

Splenic artery -> delivers blood to the spleen associated with the white pulp. The white pulp lymphoid cells monitor the blood for antigens, bacteria and foreign material if they are found the T - cells and B- cells elicit an immune response. 

Splenic vein -> blood returns to the circulation by this one and is associated with red pulp. Formed elements are stored in the splenic cord when more RBC are needed like when working out those RBC reenter circulation and it holds a lot of blood. Red pump is where venous drainage occurs
