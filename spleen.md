Largest lymphoid organ in the body
Location: Upper left (Left for lymphoid)
Main job:
-initiates an immune response when antigens are found in the blood
-reservoir for [[Red blood cells]] and [[platelets]]
-[[phagocytosis|phagocytizes]] bacteria and other foreign materials from the blood 
-[[phagocytosis|phagocytizes]] old defective RBC and platelets
